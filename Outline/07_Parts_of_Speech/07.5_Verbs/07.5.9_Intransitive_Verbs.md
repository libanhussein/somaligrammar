## Fal Magudbe (Intransitive Verbs)

| **Somali**                                                | **English**                                                                 |
|-----------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Fal magudbuhu** waa falku qaata yeele keliya ee aan qaadin karin layeelaha. | An **intransitive verb** is the verb that takes the subject only and does not have a direct (object). |
